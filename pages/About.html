About us

We're Spartan Clothing. Our mission is to unite the lifting community.

It's not just our goals that define us. It's what we do to achieve them. Working, sweating, and exhausting ourselves just a little more.

Our company began in 2023, from a IP class at Warwick School District with nothing but a computer, Visual Studio Code, and ambitions to hopefully pass the class. Today, we sit here with a half-functioning website that you can enjoy.

Our Spartan family consists of now over 1 person (because you're here!). We have 1 online stores stores so far, and we plan to keep it that way.
